About

I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate with the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), under the supervision of Prof. Zhenan Sun. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from China University of Petroleum (East China), in 2016. My current research interests include computer vision and machine learning, as well as their applications in face analysis, face generation, face recognition, etc.
